# Re-engaging Mobile Web Users with Push Notifications

## Key Use Case: Increasing Revenue via Push Notifications

This case study focuses on how Beyond the Rack leveraged web-based push notifications to re-engage users on their mobile website. The primary goal was to increase revenue per visit by providing a direct and timely channel for communication, mirroring some of the benefits of native apps.

## Best Practices

*   **Implement Push Notifications for Mobile Re-engagement:** Use push notifications to bring native-app-like capabilities to the mobile web, allowing direct communication with users who have opted in.
*   **Drive Higher Revenue:** Push notifications can significantly increase revenue per visit by bringing users back to the site for specific offers or flash sales. Beyond the Rack saw a **26%** increase in spend from users arriving via push notifications.
*   **Improve Click-Through Rates:** Push notifications offer a direct line to users, resulting in higher engagement and click-through rates (CTR) compared to traditional channels like email. Beyond the Rack achieved a **20%** CTR from push notifications.
*   **Increase Time on Site:** Users arriving via push notifications tend to spend more time on the site, indicating higher engagement and a greater propensity to browse and purchase. Members visiting via push spent **72%** more time on the site per visit.
*   **Leverage for Flash Sales:** The timely nature of push notifications makes them ideal for alerting users to time-sensitive events like flash sales, driving immediate traffic and conversions.
*   **Personalize and Add Value:** Craft push notifications that are timely and relevant to the user's interests to maximize their impact and customer satisfaction.

## Implementation Details

### How Push Notifications Work on the Web

Push notifications allow your mobile web users to opt-in to receive notifications directly on their device, similar to a native app experience. This provides a powerful channel for re-engaging users with customized and compelling content.

### Key Metrics for Success

*   **Mobile Web User Percentage:** Understand the proportion of your audience that primarily accesses your site via mobile web.
*   **Revenue Increase per Visit:** Track the direct impact of push notifications on average customer spending.
*   **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Measure the effectiveness of your push notification messaging in driving users back to the site.
*   **Time Spent on Site:** Analyze user engagement by comparing the time spent on the site by users who arrived via push versus other channels.
*   **Repeat Visits:** Monitor the increase in repeat user sessions attributed to push notification campaigns.

## Considerations for Developers

*   **User Consent:** Always obtain explicit user consent before sending push notifications.
*   **Frequency Capping:** Avoid overwhelming users with too many notifications. Implement strategies to manage notification frequency.
*   **Content Relevance:** Ensure that the content of your push notifications is valuable and relevant to the user to maintain engagement and trust.
*   **Platform Support:** Be aware of the browser and operating system support for web push notifications.

# Promotional Images

Promotional images are crucial for capturing users' attention and encouraging them to learn more about your extension. They should primarily communicate your extension's brand rather than just being a screenshot.

## Image Requirements

*   **Small Promotional Image:** 440x280 pixels (required). Extensions without this image will be listed after those that have it.
*   **Marquee Image:** 1400x560 pixels (optional).

**Note:** Promotional images are not locale-specific. If your extension supports multiple languages, consider avoiding text and focusing on graphical representations, or target the images to your largest user locale.

## Best Practices

*   **Avoid Text:** Focus on visual communication of your brand and extension's capabilities.
*   **Scalability:** Ensure your image looks good even when shrunk to half size.
*   **Background:** Assume the image will be displayed on a light gray background.
*   **Color:** Use saturated colors for better impact. Avoid excessive use of white and light gray.
*   **Composition:** Fill the entire image region and ensure well-defined edges.
*   **Brand Alignment:** If your images refer to Google brands, adhere to the [Branding Guidelines](https://developer.chrome.com/docs/webstore/branding).

## Review Status

You can check the review status of your promotional images in your developer dashboard under the 'Edit' section for your item.

*   **Pending Review:** The image is awaiting review and is not yet displayed. Review typically takes about a week.
*   **No Status:** The image has been approved and is displayed. (Note: Images for 'Draft' or 'Trusted testers' items may not show a status until the item is published.)
*   **Rejected:** The image was reviewed and rejected. Reasons for rejection will be provided, and you should address them before re-uploading.

## Replacing Images

To replace a promotional image, hover over the existing image in the developer dashboard to reveal the remove control.

## Next Steps

After preparing your promotional images, consider [Submitting your extension for publishing](https://developer.chrome.com/docs/webstore/publish/#submit-your-item-for-publishing).

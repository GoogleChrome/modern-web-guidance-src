# Registering as a Chrome Web Store Developer

To publish items on the Chrome Web Store, you must first register as a developer and pay a one-time fee. This process ensures you are a legitimate developer and allows you to distribute your applications or extensions.

## Key Considerations for Your Developer Account

When creating your developer account, several factors are important to consider:

*   **Email Address:** It is highly recommended to use a new, dedicated email account for your Chrome Web Store developer activities. This email will be used for important communications regarding your extensions, including alerts and announcements. It also simplifies account management, especially if you need to delete or transfer accounts.
*   **Account Uniqueness:** After an account is created, its associated email address cannot be changed. If you need to update your email, you will have to create a new account and transfer your existing Chrome Web Store items to it.
*   **Reusing Email Identities:** If you have previously deleted your Chrome Web Store developer account, you cannot reuse the same email identity to create a new account.
*   **Google Play Integration:** If you host your item on Google Play and wish for your Chrome Web Store listing to display an "Available for Android" link, ensure your item has the same name as your Google Play listing, and both must be owned by the same developer account.

## Registration Process

The registration process is straightforward and can be accessed through the Chrome Web Store developer dashboard.

1.  **Access the Developer Dashboard:** Navigate to the [developer dashboard][dashboard].
2.  **Agree to Terms:** On your first visit, you will be presented with the developer agreement and policies. Review and accept these terms.
3.  **Pay Registration Fee:** Complete the one-time registration fee payment.

Upon successful payment and agreement to the terms, this registration screen will not appear again.

**Warning:** If you accidentally requested the deletion of your account, please [contact developer support][one-stop] immediately.

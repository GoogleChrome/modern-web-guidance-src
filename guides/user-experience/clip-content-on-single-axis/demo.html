Creative Breakouts

Combining horizontal clipping with vertical visual continuity.

Modern Layout Strategy

In this hero section, the large glowing blob circles out from the right corner. Its width makes it bleed significantly past the right edge of the viewport, which would normally trigger a horizontal scrollbar.

Setting overflow-x: clip on the hero prevents that scrollbar by cropping the blob's width to the screen. Simultaneously, overflow-y: visible allows the bottom 80px of that same blob to "break out" and overlap this body content, creating a layered, premium feel.

Try toggling to overflow: hidden in the control panel. Notice how it solves the horizontal scroll but shears the bottom of the blob perfectly flat, breaking the design's illusion.

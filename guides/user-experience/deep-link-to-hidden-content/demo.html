Deep linking with hidden="until-found"

The native <details> element is great for general content, but it cannot be used to group and hide elements inside a different part of the DOM tree. In these structural cases, hidden="until-found" is required.

Afrobeat Music

Afrobeat is a music genre that involves the combination of elements of West African musical styles such as fuji music and highlife with American jazz and later soul and funk influences, with a focus on chanted vocals, complex intersecting rhythms, and percussion.

The term was popularized in the 1960s by Nigerian multi-instrumentalist and bandleader Fela Kuti, who is responsible for pioneering and popularizing the style both within and outside Nigeria.

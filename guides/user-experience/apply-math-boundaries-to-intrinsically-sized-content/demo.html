Clamping & Ellipsis with calc-size()

This sidebar dynamically adjusts to its content but enforces a strict 35vw limit.

Current Rule:
width: calc-size(fit-content, clamp(180px, size, 35vw));

When the name becomes too long, calc-size stops expanding the parent, and the child's text-overflow: ellipsis kicks in.

Note: Hover the sidebar to expand it. Try resizing your browser window while the "Very Long Name" is active to see the ellipsis respond dynamically to the 35vw limit.
